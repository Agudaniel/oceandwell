What is the Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, & Bitcoin Cash confirmation?

All Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, & Bitcoin Cash operations are confirmed by a Blockchain network. Every single computer in the Blockchain network confirms your transaction, increasing the total number of confirmations. When the number of confirmations reaches a specific threshold, usually from 3 to 6, the funds appear in the recipient account. Usually, such a process takes from up to 30 minutes.
